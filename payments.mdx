## Merchant of Record

Pandabase acts as the **Merchant of Record (MoR)** for all transactions. This means we process payments, handle tax collection, manage disputes, and issue refunds on your behalf. Your customers pay Pandabase — we settle funds to your available balance after deducting fees.

## Currency

All settlements are in **USD**. Regardless of where your store or customers are located, your balance and payouts are always in US dollars.

## Payment Methods

Customers can pay with:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Additional methods depending on the customer's region

## Fees

| Fee          | Amount                                              |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Platform fee | 5.9% + $0.20 per transaction                        |
| Dispute fee  | $20.00 per dispute                                  |
| Payout fee   | $1.50 base + [country-specific fees](/payouts#fees) |

<Note>
  There are no monthly fees, setup fees, or hidden charges. You only pay when
  you make a sale.
</Note>

## How a payment works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer checks out">
    A customer adds items to a checkout session and enters their payment
    details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment is processed">
    The customer's card is charged and the payment is verified.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order is created">
    On successful payment, an order is created and fulfillment is triggered
    automatically (license keys, downloads, webhooks, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance is updated">
    The sale amount (minus platform fees) is added to your store's available
    balance. You can request a [payout](/payouts) at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order statuses

| Status     | Description                                              |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| PENDING    | Payment intent created, waiting for customer             |
| PROCESSING | Payment is being processed                               |
| COMPLETED  | Payment succeeded, order fulfilled                       |
| FAILED     | Payment failed (card declined, insufficient funds, etc.) |
| CANCELLED  | Payment was cancelled before completion                  |
| REFUNDED   | Order was refunded                                       |
| CHARGEBACK | Customer filed a dispute with their bank                 |
