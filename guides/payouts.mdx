## Overview

Pandabase handles payouts to your bank account directly. Once you've verified your identity and added a bank account, you can request payouts from your available balance.

## Minimum Payout

The minimum payout amount is **$50.00** for all countries and three times a week.

## Fees

Every payout has a **$1.50 base fee**. If your bank account is outside the US, additional cross-border and currency conversion fees apply.

You can preview the exact fees before requesting a payout using the **estimate** feature in the dashboard or via the API.

## New bank accounts

When you add a bank account for the first time, it enters an **In Review** status for 2 to 3 business days. During this period, Pandabase verifies your bank details and runs compliance checks. You cannot request payouts until the review is complete.

You will receive an email when your bank account is approved and ready to accept payouts.

### First payout waiting period

After your bank account is approved, there is a **7 to 14 day waiting period** before your first payout is settled. This delay is necessary for risk mitigation and cannot be waived under any circumstances.

| Event                                    | Timeline                              |
| ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Bank account added                       | Day 0                                 |
| Bank account approved (In Review clears) | Day 2 to 3                            |
| First payout requested                   | Day 3+                                |
| First payout settled                     | 7 to 14 days after the payout request |

After your first payout settles successfully, all future payouts follow the standard timeline below.

<Warning>
  The first payout waiting period applies to every new bank account, even if you
  have previously received payouts to a different account. Changing your bank
  details resets the waiting period.
</Warning>
