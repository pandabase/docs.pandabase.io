## Overview

A store is where your products live. Each store has its own products, orders, balance, webhooks, API tokens, and team members. You can create up to **5 stores** per account.

## Creating a store

When creating a store, you'll provide:

- **Name** — your store's display name
- **Summary** — a short description shown on your storefront
- **Description** — a longer description for your store page
- **Category** — what you sell (software, digital products, ebooks, courses, etc.)
- **Support email** — where customers can reach you
- **Origin country** — your country of operation

## Storefront

Every store gets a public storefront where customers can browse your products and categories. The storefront is accessible without authentication and supports:

- Store info (name, logo, description, colors)
- Product listings with search and filtering
- Category browsing with hierarchy
- Individual product pages with variants and options

## Customization

You can customize your storefront's appearance with:

- **Logo** and **favicon**
- **Accent color** and **primary color**
- **Social links** (Twitter, Discord, etc.)

## Team members

Invite team members to help manage your store. Each member has a role that controls what they can do.

| Role          | Permissions                                                        |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Viewer        | Read-only access to orders, products, and analytics                |
| Moderator     | Everything a viewer can do, plus manage products and orders        |
| Developer     | Everything a moderator can do, plus manage webhooks and API tokens |
| Administrator | Full access including team management, payouts, and store settings |

<Note>
  Only the store owner can enable payouts, manage bank accounts, and delete the
  store.
</Note>

## Store requirements

Before your store can accept payments, you need:

1. A verified account (KYC completed)
2. A verified phone number
3. At least one active product
4. Payouts enabled with a bank account (to receive funds)
