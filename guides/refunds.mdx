## Overview

As the merchant of record, Pandabase processes refunds on your behalf. You can issue a full refund for any completed order within 30 days of the original payment.

## Requirements

To issue a refund, all of the following must be true:

- The order status is **COMPLETED**
- No existing refund is pending or completed for the order
- The order has no dispute history
- The order is within **30 days** of payment
- Your store has sufficient available balance to cover the refund amount

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate refund">
    Request a refund through the dashboard or API, with an optional reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance check">
    Pandabase verifies your store has enough available balance to cover the
    refund.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refund processed">
    The refund is issued to the customer's original payment method. Your store
    balance is decremented by the refund amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer notified">
    The customer receives a refund confirmation email. Funds typically appear in
    their account within 5–10 business days depending on their bank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Refund reasons

| Reason                | Description                         |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Requested by customer | Customer asked for a refund         |
| Duplicate             | Customer was charged more than once |
| Fraudulent            | The charge was not authorized       |

<Note>
  If a coupon was used on the order, the coupon usage is rolled back when the
  refund completes — so the coupon can be used again.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Platform fees are not refunded. If a customer paid $100 and you refund the
  full amount, your balance is decremented by the full $100 but the original
  platform fee is not returned.
</Warning>
