## Block confirmations

The number of block confirmations required before a payment is considered final varies by chain:

| Chain        | Confirmations | Approximate time |
| ------------ | ------------- | ---------------- |
| Bitcoin      | 1             | ~10 minutes      |
| Ethereum     | 12            | ~3 minutes       |
| Solana       | 32            | ~15 seconds      |
| NEAR         | 5             | ~5 seconds       |
| Base         | 12            | ~30 seconds      |
| Arbitrum     | 12            | ~15 seconds      |
| Optimism     | 12            | ~30 seconds      |
| Polygon      | 32            | ~1 minute        |
| Scroll       | 12            | ~3 minutes       |
| BSC          | 15            | ~45 seconds      |
| Avalanche    | 12            | ~30 seconds      |
| TON          | 5             | ~15 seconds      |
| Tron         | 20            | ~1 minute        |
| Sui          | 3             | ~2 seconds       |
| Aptos        | 3             | ~2 seconds       |
| XRP Ledger   | 1             | ~4 seconds       |
| Cardano      | 15            | ~5 minutes       |
| Stellar      | 1             | ~5 seconds       |
| Dogecoin     | 6             | ~6 minutes       |
| Litecoin     | 6             | ~15 minutes      |
| Bitcoin Cash | 6             | ~60 minutes      |

<Warning>
  Confirmation times are approximate and depend on network congestion. During
  high traffic, confirmations may take longer than listed.
</Warning>
