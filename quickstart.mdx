---
title: "Start selling"
description: "Start selling digital products within minutes of signing up."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [merchant.pandabase.io](https://merchant.pandabase.io/auth/signup) with your email. We'll send you a magic link to verify your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Click the magic link and finish your profile (username, first and last name, phone number, country), then accept our legal policies.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch your store">
    Tell us about your store: name, URL, statement descriptor, support email, country of operation, category, description, and what you're selling. Your storefront goes live as soon as you submit.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/guides/launch-your-store.png" alt="Store creation form" />
    </Frame>

    <Accordion title="Supported countries">
      **Americas:** 🇨🇦 Canada, 🇺🇸 United States

      **Europe:** 🇦🇹 Austria, 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇭🇷 Croatia, 🇨🇾 Cyprus, 🇨🇿 Czech Republic, 🇩🇰 Denmark, 🇪🇪 Estonia, 🇫🇮 Finland, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇬🇧 Great Britain, 🇬🇷 Greece, 🇭🇺 Hungary, 🇮🇪 Ireland, 🇮🇹 Italy, 🇱🇻 Latvia, 🇱🇹 Lithuania, 🇱🇺 Luxembourg, 🇲🇹 Malta, 🇳🇱 Netherlands, 🇳🇴 Norway, 🇵🇱 Poland, 🇵🇹 Portugal, 🇷🇴 Romania, 🇸🇰 Slovakia, 🇸🇮 Slovenia, 🇪🇸 Spain, 🇸🇪 Sweden, 🇨🇭 Switzerland, 🇹🇷 Turkiye

      **Asia-Pacific:** 🇦🇺 Australia, 🇭🇰 Hong Kong, 🇮🇳 India, 🇳🇿 New Zealand, 🇵🇭 Philippines, 🇸🇬 Singapore, 🇹🇼 Taiwan, 🇹🇭 Thailand, 🇻🇳 Vietnam

      **Middle East:** 🇮🇱 Israel, 🇶🇦 Qatar, 🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates

      **Africa:** 🇲🇦 Morocco
    </Accordion>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    Submit your ID (handled by Sumsub; Pandabase never stores it) and verify your phone number with 2FA. You can start accepting payments immediately, but KYC is required before we release payouts.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/guides/identity-verification.png" alt="Identity verification screen" />
    </Frame>

    <Note>
      **Why do we need your ID?**

      Your ID lets us confirm who we're sending money to, as required by anti-money-laundering and know-your-customer (KYC) laws in every country we operate in (in the US, that's the Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act). We don't store your ID ourselves. It's processed by Sumsub, a third-party identity verification platform we use to combat fraud and stay compliant. It isn't shared or used beyond that.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the store questionnaire">
    A short business questionnaire appears as a banner at the top of your dashboard. A few questions about your business model, takes a couple of minutes.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/guides/store-questionnaire.png" alt="Store questionnaire banner" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start accepting payments">
    Share your storefront link or integrate the [Checkout API](/developers/learn/hosted-checkout) to take your first payment. We handle tax, disputes, and payouts automatically. Funds land in your bank account on your chosen schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>
