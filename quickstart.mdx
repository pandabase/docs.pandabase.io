---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Start selling digital products within minutes of signing up."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [pandabase.io](https://pandabase.io) using your email. You'll
    receive a magic link to verify your account — no passwords needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    Complete identity verification (KYC) and verify your phone number. This is
    required before you can accept payments or receive payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a store">
    Set up your store with a name, description, category, and support email. You
    can create up to 3 stores per account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add products">
    Create products with pricing, images, and fulfillment settings. Pandabase
    supports digital downloads, license keys, webhooks, redirects, and manual
    fulfillment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up payouts">
    Enable payouts and add your bank account. We support [30+
    countries](/payouts) with local bank transfers and SWIFT.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start selling">
    Share your storefront link or integrate with the [Checkout
    API](/developers/webhooks/overview) to start accepting payments. Pandabase
    handles tax, disputes, and payouts automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
