## Overview

Pandabase handles payouts to your bank account directly. Once you've verified your identity and added a bank account, you can request payouts from your available balance.

## Minimum Payout

The minimum payout amount is **$50.00** for all countries.

## Fees

Every payout has a **$1.50 base fee**. If your bank account is outside the US, additional cross-border and currency conversion fees apply.

You can preview the exact fees before requesting a payout using the **estimate** feature in the dashboard or via the API.

### Fee breakdown by country

| Country | Base Fee | Cross-Border | FX | Example ($100 payout) |
|---------|----------|-------------|-----|----------------------|
| US | $1.50 | — | — | $1.50 total |
| GB | $1.50 | 0.25% | 0.5% | $2.25 total |
| EU (Eurozone) | $1.50 | 0.25% | 0.5% | $2.25 total |
| CA | $1.50 | 0.25% | 1% | $2.75 total |
| SE, NO, CZ, HU | $1.50 | 0.25% | 1% | $2.75 total |
| DK, PL | $1.50 | 0.5% | 1% | $3.00 total |
| SG | $1.50 | 0.5% | 1% | $3.00 total |
| TR | $1.50 | 0.75% | 1% | $3.25 total |

<Note>
  Cross-border and FX fees are percentage-based and calculated on the payout amount. All fees are deducted from your available balance at the time of the request.
</Note>

## Supported Countries

| Country | Method | Currency | Bank Fields |
|---------|--------|----------|-------------|
| US | ACH | USD | Routing number, account number, account type |
| GB | Faster Payments | GBP | Sort code, account number |
| CA | EFT | CAD | Transit number, institution number, account number, account type |
| SG | Local transfer | SGD | Bank code, branch code, account number |
| TR | SWIFT | TRY | SWIFT/BIC code, IBAN, account type |
| AT, BE, CY, DE, EE, ES, FI, FR, GR, HR, IE, IT, LT, LU, LV, MT, NL, PT, SI, SK | SEPA | EUR | IBAN |
| CZ, DK, HU, NO, PL, SE | SEPA | EUR | IBAN |

## Payout Timeline

- **US (ACH)**: 1–2 business days
- **GB (Faster Payments)**: Same day or next business day
- **EU/EEA (SEPA)**: 1–2 business days
- **CA (EFT)**: 2–3 business days
- **SG (Local)**: 1–2 business days
- **TR (SWIFT)**: 3–5 business days

<Note>
  Payout requests may be subject to review. You'll receive an email with the status of your request.
</Note>
