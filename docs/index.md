---
title: "What is Pandabase?"
description: "Welcome to Pandabase — the merchant of record platform for digital commerce."
---

Pandabase is a merchant of record (MoR) platform that handles payments, tax, disputes, and payouts on behalf of your store. You focus on building great products and we will handle the rest.
